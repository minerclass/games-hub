Featured visual simulations

Pedagogical friction made visible.

These two simulations turn the framework into a visual decision space. Load-Bearing focuses on the difference between polished artifacts and durable learning. Trailhead focuses on productive struggle, access barriers, and equitable scaffolding.

2026 Featured simulation ⏱️ ~15 min 🎓 Teacher PD Pedagogical friction / instructional design

Load-Bearing

Build a unit; watch it become a bridge. Structural thinking holds the transfer load, while polish alone shears off. An X-ray reveals the difference between the artifact of learning and the learning itself.

Play Load-Bearing Flagship visual simulation for unproductive success, process evidence, and the artifact-versus-learning distinction.
2026 Featured simulation ⏱️ ~15 min 🎓 Teacher PD Pedagogical friction / equity / scaffolding

Trailhead

One trail, four hikers. Watch productive struggle and exclusionary barriers shape who reaches the summit, then redesign the route so every learner can climb without bypassing the work.

Play Trailhead Equity-focused companion simulation for calibrated scaffolding, device bans, AI bypass, and access barriers.
2026 Pedagogical friction ⏱️ ~10 min 🏫 Grade 9-12 Reconstruction unit / Unproductive success

The Schema Constructor

Construct a 6-lesson unit on the Reconstruction era. Choose between scaffolded manual inquiry and effortless AI shortcuts, and discover how hollow polish fails under transfer stress.

Play The Schema Constructor Visual simulation illustrating active cognitive connection building vs. AI-bypass.
2026 Pedagogical friction ⏱️ ~10 min 🏫 Grade 9-12 Argumentation / Existential Authorship

The Dialectical Crucible

Construct a vertical argument tower to support your thesis. Defend your evidence joints, and see if your stone foundations can withstand objection catapults that shatter gold-clad AI shortcuts.

Play The Dialectical Crucible Interactive physics blueprint game on evidence grounding and structural integrity.
2026 Media ecology ⏱️ ~10 min 🏫 Grade 6-12 Source validation / lateral reading

The Algorithmic Stream

Navigate a flowing river of historical and generative information. Use Friction Dams to slow the current and Corroboration Lenses to expose fabricated sources before your bibliography fails the audit.

Play The Algorithmic Stream Flowing river simulation for lateral reading, source audits, and tertiary algorithmicity.
2026 Historical inquiry ⏱️ ~15 min 🏫 Grade 8-12 Rhetorical friction / AI

The Devil's Advocate

An AI argues fluent, one-sided historical theses; players rebut with sourcing, context, corroboration, and counter-evidence — and learn when to push back and when to concede a fair point.

Play The Devil's Advocate Pairs with the "devil's-advocate bot" move and the rhetorical-friction dimension of the deck.
2026 Pedagogical friction ⏱️ ~15 min 🎓 Teacher PD Teacher PD / C.O.R.E. & H.E.A.R.T.

Common Ground: The Polarized Classroom

Step into the teacher's chair: navigate charged classroom moments with C.O.R.E. and H.E.A.R.T., balancing civil discourse, academic rigor, and student trust.

Play Common Ground Best for teacher PD and the "balanced history in a polarized world" session.
2026 Historical inquiry ⏱️ ~10 min 🏫 Grade 6-12 Social studies / AI

Keepers of Inquiry

Players respond to a polished AI-generated historical claim by choosing inquiry moves that protect sourcing, context, corroboration, agency, and access.

Play Keepers of Inquiry Best paired with the NCSS historical inquiry deck and algorithm-audit activity.
2026 Historical inquiry ⏱️ ~20 min 🏫 Grade 8-12 Contextualization / anti-presentism

Out of Time

Four charged moments — 1774 Boston, 1787 Philadelphia, 1961 Anniston, 1943 Manzanar. Assemble what actors could actually know, dodge anachronism and hindsight traps, and appraise hard decisions without presentism or easy exoneration.

Play Out of Time Best for historical empathy, contextualization, and "should we judge the past?" discussions.
2026 Civic inquiry ⏱️ ~15 min 🏫 Grade 8-12 C3 / informed action / AI

Informed Action

Take a civic issue from question to proposal: gather evidence, map stakeholders, resist frictionless AI shortcuts, and audit the machine-drafted plan before your name goes on it.

Play Informed Action Pairs with C3 Dimension 4 (taking informed action) and AI-policy conversations — watch the Owned / Borrowed / Outsourced chip.
2026 Source literacy ⏱️ ~10 min 🏫 Grade 6-12 Historical thinking

Keeper of the Source

A historical inquiry simulator focused on source judgment, evidence, and the interpretive moves that make historical claims defensible.

Play Keeper of the Source Use when the learning goal is sourcing, verification, and careful evidence work.
2026 Friction ⏱️ ~10 min 🎓 Teacher PD Instructional design

Friction Lab

A calibration challenge for distinguishing productive friction, exclusionary friction, and context-dependent difficulty in learning design.

Play Friction Lab Best for workshops on pedagogical friction, AI policy, and instructional design.
2026 Media ecology ⏱️ ~15 min 🎓 Higher Ed Ong / orality

Keeper of the Word

A playable media-ecology journey through the shifting sensorium, helping players feel how communication technologies reorganize thought and culture.

Play Keeper of the Word Use with media ecology, Ong, secondary orality, and tertiary algorithmicity conversations.
